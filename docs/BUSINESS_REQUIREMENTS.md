# Business Requirements

## Objective
System for:
- Sales
- Purchasing
- Inventory
- Serial Tracking
- Warranty

## Modules
- Sales (Quotation, Invoice, Receipt, AR)
- Purchasing (PR, PO, Receiving, AP)
- Inventory
- Serial Tracking
- Warranty

## Key Rules
- Some items require serial tracking
- Serial must be unique
- Cannot sell same serial twice
- Warranty starts when sold
